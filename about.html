About Us
Pocket Somm is the brainchild of three web dev bootcamp students, who found a common ground in their love for wine and food. The name comes from the idea of having a sommelier (wine expert) on hand to help with selecting a wine to go with a specific food. We used openAI to get the wine pairings, and Spoonacular to get the wine descriptions.
